Cases Involving Body Fluid Evidence

Body fluid evidence, most often found in the form of blood, is the most disturbing type of evidence found at a crime scene as it indicates that a violent crime has occurred. A great deal of information about the activities that occurred during the crime or about the perpetrator can be determined from body fluid evidence. Body fluid evidence can be considered to be one of the most valuable types of evidence that exists.
