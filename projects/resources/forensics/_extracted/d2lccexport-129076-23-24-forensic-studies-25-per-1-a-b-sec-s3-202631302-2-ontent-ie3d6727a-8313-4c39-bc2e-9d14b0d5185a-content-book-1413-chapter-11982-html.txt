One of the most useful tools that forensic experts use to collect trace evidence is a common household item - clear tape. The technique is simple: a piece of wide clear tape is placed firmly onto an object and then removed. Any loose debris or trace evidence that sticks to the tape is analyzed under the microscope.
This technique is so easy that you can try it at home. A magnifying glass works well enough because you are unlikely to be solving a heinous crime! You may be amazed at what you find. If you can work with a friend or sibling, some imagination and brainstorming could produce a lot of fun, too.
